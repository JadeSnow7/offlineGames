### Description

Offline Games brings six classic and original mini-games into one clean, fast, and privacy-first app.

Included games:
- Snake: Grow longer, dodge walls, and beat your best score.
- Block Puzzle: Drop and rotate pieces to clear lines.
- Breakout: Control the paddle, break bricks, protect your lives.
- Minesweeper: Reveal safe cells, flag mines, and solve the board.
- Memory Match: Flip cards and clear all matching pairs.
- Reaction Tap: Tap at the right moment and test your reflex speed.

Why players like it:
- Fully offline gameplay.
- No ads, no in-app purchases, no account required.
- English and Simplified Chinese.
- Local high scores for every game.
- Built-in pause, restart, and quick resume.
- Sound and haptic toggles.
- Designed for iPhone portrait play.

Privacy:
Offline Games does not collect, share, or track any personal data. Game settings and scores stay on your device.
